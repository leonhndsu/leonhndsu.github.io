CH.6: Row Striping



What: Use shades and alternate the background with the shades in table rows.

When: Use to look up specific data items or looking through a table and make it easier to search through it.

Why: Use to distinguish what to look for in a table. To make the viewers to read the row individually instead in masses.

How: To use ‘row stripping”, You need at least 2 shades of color and then implement the shade as stripes in the table.
