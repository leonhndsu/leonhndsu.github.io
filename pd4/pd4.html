CH.9: Social Links



What: A group of links of all the social media accounts you have.

When: To support your social media channels and let them go visit other websites.

Why: To promote the social channels you have and help create more media presence. It shows that you are up to knowledge with technology.

How: Have a section for all the social media links and link it with a clickable icon.
